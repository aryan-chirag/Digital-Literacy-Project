Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

✅ DO’s:
1. Maintain a professional and respectful online presence.   
2. Think carefully before posting any content.  
3. Use privacy settings to protect personal information.   
4. Share useful, informative, and positive content.   
5. Be polite and respectful in all online interaction  

🚫DON’Ts:
1. Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.   
2. Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content.  
3. Do not spread misinformation or fake news.  
4. Avoid engaging in unnecessary online arguments.   
5. Do not use abusive or disrespectful language.   

------------------------------------------------------------

📊 Quick Checklist

| DO’s | DON’Ts |
|------|--------|
| Maintain professionalism | Avoid sharing sensitive information |
| Think before posting | Avoid offensive content |
| Use privacy settings | Do not spread fake news |
| Share useful content | Avoid arguments |
| Be respectful online | Avoid abusive language |

------------------------------------------------------------

🧠 Conclusion:

 By following these do’s and avoiding the don’ts, students can use social media in a safe, respectful, and productive manner.
