# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist

## 📌 General Safety Tips
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible   
- Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links   
- Keep your apps and devices updated regularly  
- Do not share personal information on untrusted websites  

## 💳 Financial & UPI Safety Tips
- Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be bank officials   
- Always verify payment requests carefully before approving them  
- Remember: You do NOT need to enter a PIN to receive money   

## 🎓 Tips for Students
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for transactions or sensitive logins   
- Be cautious of fake internship or job offers asking for money  
- Download apps only from trusted sources like Play Store or App Store
- Do not share your passwords or login details with friends  
- Always log out from shared or public devices      

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
- Report incidents on the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for immediate assistance   

## 🔒 Conclusion
Following these safety practices can help prevent cyber fraud and protect your personal and financial information. Staying aware and cautious is the key to staying safe online.
